About Viva Kids World – Enriching Young Minds at Play School!

Where fun and learning come together

At Viva Kids World Preschool, we provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where young minds embark on an exciting journey of learning. Our innovative, play-based curriculum fosters creativity, curiosity, and all-round development, ensuring that every child thrives.

Our experienced educators focus on a holistic learning approach that balances academics, arts, and physical activities. Through interactive sessions, hands-on activities, and structured playtime, we create an atmosphere that promotes self-confidence and independence in young learners.

At Viva Kids World, we prioritize every child’s emotional and cognitive growth, ensuring they develop the necessary skills to transition seamlessly into formal education.



Our Philosophy – Learn, Grow, and Enjoy!



Nurturing curiosity, confidence, and lifelong learning

At Viva Kids World, we firmly believe that every child is unique and deserves an educational experience that nurtures their individuality while making learning an enjoyable journey. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed, blending Montessori principles with modern teaching techniques to ensure a holistic approach to early childhood education. We focus on fostering emotional, cognitive, social, and physical development, creating a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Through engaging activities and guided interactions, we encourage children to express their emotions, develop resilience, and build empathy, allowing them to form meaningful connections with their peers. Our approach also stimulates cognitive growth by incorporating problem-solving activities, critical thinking exercises, and hands-on learning experiences that ignite curiosity and a love for exploration.

Empowering Your Child’s Journey from the First Step

We offer four structured programs—Playgroup, Nursery, Jr.KG, and Sr.KG—designed to cater to different age groups and learning needs. Each program follows a child-centered approach, allowing children to learn at their own pace while gaining confidence in their abilities. Our classrooms are filled with vibrant learning materials and age-appropriate resources, ensuring an enriching experience that promotes independence and self-expression.

Achivements

At Viva Kids World, we are committed to providing a holistic and enriching learning experience that nurtures every aspect of a child's development—academically, socially, emotionally, and creatively.
